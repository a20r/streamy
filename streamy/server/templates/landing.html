What is Streamy?

Streamy is a crowd-sourced journalism platform. With streamy, you can view live broadcasts of popular current events provided by nearby users. Becoming a streamy broadcaster is easy, it takes seconds! Just click "Broadcast" and begin broadcasting straight away, on any device. When you make a tweet near a place covered by major news networks, streamy will tweet you a link to begin streaming unbiased content to other users. Discover more!
